Sugar Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks of softened butter.
  • 3½ cups of sifted flour
  • 1t of backing powder
  • ½t salt
  • 1½ cups of sugar
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 1½t of vanilla

Directions

Cream butter and sugar then add the beaten eggs and vanilla + the rest of the ingredients. Bake at 400° on a greased or non stick cooking sheet.


Icing: ¾ cup of powdered sugar + ¼ cup of cream (or milk) + 1T of butter. If its took thick add a little more cream (or milk) for spreading and vice versa, if its too runny add more powdered sugar
